Ecommerce, Order, Pick & Pack Fufilment

We get an order delivered to a customer after they order it online. This we achieve by receiving and storing inventory, processing orders, picking items, packing boxes, and transporting the items to the customer's shipping destination.

Our Methods Of Fufilment

Our methods depends on customers preferences and product types.
  • Outsourced
  • Dropshipping
  • Hybrid
  • We also deploy Softwares to help take accurate stocks,which are upgraded from time to time to meet our Warehousing and fulfillment needs.
