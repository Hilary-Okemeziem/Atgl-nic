3PL(FBA), Inventory planning, Warehouse services

for your specific challenges

Our THIRD PARTY LOGISTICS SERVICES helps our clients to scale space, labor, and transportation according to current inventory.

Inventory Planning

Our Inventory planning includes creating forecasts to determine how much inventory should be on hand to meet consumer demand. We also apply Inventory control,by which our managers count and maintain inventory items in the business.

Warehouse Storage Services

We apply Pallet Racking,Shelving, Mobile Shelving, Multi-Tier Racking and Mezzanine Flooring in our Warehouse system,depending on type of goods been stored or handled.
We also deploy Softwares to help take accurate stocks,which are upgraded from time to time to meet our Warehousing and fulfillment needs.
What Motivates The Choice Of Business/Philosophy Of Amber To Go Logistics Company
Our objective is to ensure Material Availability to businesses, Better Level of Customer Service, Keeping Wastage and Losses to a Minimum and Maintaining Sufficient Stock for our partners by providing them Cost-Effective Storage, making sure Cost Value of Inventories Can Be Reduced and Optimizing Product Sales.
